Discord CBL Bot - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 September, 2024

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Discord CBL Bot ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our bot service.

2. Information We Collect

When you use the Discord CBL Bot, we may collect the following information:

  • SteamIDs provided in queries
  • Community Ban List (CBL) data associated with queried SteamIDs
  • Discord user IDs of users who initiate queries
  • Discord server IDs where the bot is used

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide CBL history and ban information for queried SteamIDs
  • Improve and optimize the bot's performance and user experience
  • Comply with Discord's Developer Terms of Service and Developer Policy

4. Data Retention

We retain query logs for a period of 30 days for debugging and optimization purposes. After this period, the logs are automatically deleted.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. The bot retrieves publicly available information from the Community Ban List API.

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the information collected by the bot. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you and to request its deletion. To exercise these rights, please contact us through our GitHub discussions page.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us on our GitHub discussions page at https://github.com/Frontline-Femmes/discord-cbl/discussions.
